UniTracko — Legal & Policies

Privacy Policy

1. Who We Are

UniTracko is a platform that helps students and parents navigate college admissions in India.

We are operated by Unitracko Private Limited, registered under the Companies Act, 2013 (India).

2. Laws We Follow

We follow all applicable Indian laws, including:

  • Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act)
  • DPDP Rules, 2025 (notified on 13 Nov 2025)
  • Information Technology Act, 2000 and IT (SPDI) Rules, 2011
  • Consumer Protection Act, 2019

We are building full compliance ahead of the May 2027 DPDP deadline.

3. What Data We Collect

Information you give us:

  • Name, email, phone number
  • Academic details (class, marks, interests, colleges)
  • Parent/guardian details (if shared)
  • Messages or queries

Automatically collected:

  • Device, browser, IP address
  • Pages visited, time spent, usage patterns
  • Referral source
  • Cookies (explained below)

We do NOT collect:

  • Aadhaar, PAN (unless legally required)
  • Card/bank details (handled by payment providers)
  • Health or biometric data

We follow data minimisation under the DPDP Act.

4. Why We Collect Your Data

As per the DPDP Act (purpose limitation), we use data only for:

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Personalised guidance and recommendations
  • Sending updates (only with your consent)
  • Improving the platform (using anonymised data)
  • Customer support
  • Processing payments
  • Legal compliance

We will not use your data for new purposes without informing you.

5. What We Will NEVER Do

  • Sell, rent, or trade your data
  • Share your phone number for spam calls
  • Use your data for third-party advertising
  • Transfer data outside India without safeguards/consent
  • Keep your data longer than needed
  • Make important automated decisions without informing you

6. Who We Share Data With

Only when necessary:

  • Cloud providers (for hosting)
  • Analytics tools (only anonymised data)
  • Payment processors (they handle payments securely)
  • Government/legal authorities (if required by law)

All partners are bound by strict data protection agreements.

7. Consent (Your Choice)

Under the DPDP Act, your consent must be clear and voluntary.

We ensure:

  • No pre-ticked boxes
  • Separate consent for optional communication
  • Clear explanation before collecting new data

You can withdraw consent anytime: contact@unitracko.com

8. Your Rights (Under DPDP Act, 2023)

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct your data
  • Delete your data
  • Raise a complaint
  • Nominate someone to act on your behalf

If unresolved, you can approach the Data Protection Board of India.

9. Children’s Data

As per DPDP Act:

  • Users under 18 need parental consent
  • We do not track or target minors for ads
  • We do not collect data of children below 13
  • Parents can request access or deletion anytime

10. Data Retention

  • Active accounts → as long as you use them
  • Deleted accounts → removed within 30 days
  • Support data → up to 12 months
  • Analytics → anonymised, up to 24 months
  • Financial records → up to 7 years (legal requirement)

11. Data Security

We use industry-standard protection:

  • Encryption (AES-256, TLS 1.2+)
  • Restricted internal access
  • Regular security audits

If a data breach happens, we will notify you as required under the DPDP Act.

12. Cross-Border Data Transfers

Under current DPDP rules (April 2026):

  • No restricted country list is notified yet
  • If data is stored abroad, we ensure proper safeguards

Terms of Use

13. Who Can Use UniTracko

  • You must be 13+ years old
  • Under 18 → parental consent required
  • You must provide correct information

14. Your Responsibilities

You agree to:

  • Keep your account secure
  • Not share login details
  • Use the platform honestly

15. What You Can Do

  • Explore colleges and exams
  • Plan your admission journey
  • Use tools and resources
  • Contact support

16. What You Cannot Do

  • Use fake or misleading information
  • Copy or scrape platform data
  • Use bots or automation
  • Hack or disrupt the platform
  • Access other users’ data

Violation may lead to account suspension.

17. Ownership (Intellectual Property)

All content on UniTracko is protected under:

  • Copyright Act, 1957
  • Trade Marks Act, 1999

You cannot copy, reuse, or sell it without permission.

18. No Guarantee Clause (Important)

UniTracko does NOT guarantee:

  • Admission to any college
  • Accuracy of all third-party data
  • Exam success
  • Career outcomes

Final decisions are made by institutions and authorities.

19. Third-Party Links

We may link to external sites (colleges, exams, govt portals).

We are not responsible for their content or policies.

20. Platform Availability

We try to ensure uptime, but:

  • Downtime may occur
  • Maintenance may happen
  • We are not liable for losses due to this.

21. Limitation of Liability

Under Indian law, we are not responsible for:

  • Decisions based on platform information
  • Missed deadlines or opportunities
  • Indirect or financial losses

22. Governing Law

  • Governed by laws of India
  • Disputes handled in Greater Noida courts

Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Login and security
  • Performance and analytics
  • Preferences

We do NOT:

  • Track you across websites
  • Run ad targeting

You can manage cookies in browser settings.

Refund Policy

  • Free plan → no refund needed
  • Paid plans → non-refundable
  • Form services → non-refundable once work starts

Refunds only in cases like:

  • Billing errors
  • Duplicate charges
  • Service not started
